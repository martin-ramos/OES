# OES Manifesto

Engineering must be disciplined but efficient.

OES exists to:

- Enforce clarity.
- Enforce SOLID without dogma.
- Consider performance when relevant.
- Prevent silent technical debt.
- Require explicit final review.
- Measure quality.

OES rejects:

- Overengineering.
- Process for the sake of process.
- Role-play based architectures.
- Uncontrolled token growth.

OES is not a methodology.
It is a governance layer.
